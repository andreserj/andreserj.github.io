Proem

I live up in the air and I don’t belong to any country. I write because sometimes it’s the only way I understand myself. Marketing was the degree, but stories are the work. I believe in tone, texture, and timing—whether in film, design, or a line that doesn’t need to explain itself.

I grew up where language split in two. Spanish at home, English in school. That fracture still echoes in how I write, how I see, how I listen. I’m drawn to quiet things. Messy things. Honest things.

This is a place for work and thought. Not finished. Not perfect. But always true.
